# ⚡ /login & /logout — Claude Code Commands

## 📌 Purpose
`/login` and `/logout` are used to **sign in and sign out of your Anthropic account** in Claude Code.

---

## ⚙️ What They Do

### 🔹 /login
- Signs you into your Anthropic account  
- Connects Claude Code to your account  
- Enables full features and access  

---

### 🔹 /logout
- Signs you out of your account  
- Disconnects Claude Code from your account  
- Ends authenticated access  

---

## 🚀 Usage

### ✅ Login
/login

---

### ✅ Logout
/logout

---

## 💡 When to Use

### Use `/login`
- When starting Claude Code for the first time  
- When you are logged out  
- When you want to access your account features  

---

### Use `/logout`
- When you finish your work  
- When using a shared computer  
- When you want to switch accounts  

---

## 🧠 Pro Tip
Always use `/logout` on shared systems to keep your account safe.

---

## ⚠️ Notes
- You need an Anthropic account to use Claude Code  
- `/login` may open a browser for authentication  
- `/logout` does not delete your data, only signs you out  

---

## 📚 Summary
`/login` = Sign in  
`/logout` = Sign out  

---
