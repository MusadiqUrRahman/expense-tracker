Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password. As you use Spendly, we store the expense data you enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We do not collect any data beyond what is necessary to provide the service.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. This includes displaying your expenses, generating spending summaries, and enabling you to filter and manage your financial records. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal or financial data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in our database. We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services for hosting and infrastructure. These providers are bound by their own privacy policies and are only given access to information necessary to perform their services. We do not share your financial data with any third-party analytics or advertising platforms.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at support@spendly.app. We are committed to addressing your concerns promptly.
